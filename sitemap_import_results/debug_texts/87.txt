(Photo by Quinn Glabicki/PublicSource) As far as campaign funding is concerned, Democratic nominee Corey O’Connor is running away with the general election race for Pittsburgh mayor. He has raised $367,000 since June 10, new disclosures show, and has $270,000 on hand heading into the final stretch of the election season.
