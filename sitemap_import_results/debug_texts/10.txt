Inside Pittsburgh’s North Side: The people, the places, the stories This special edition zine brings together stories from across Pittsburgh’s North Side — from longtime residents and business owners to youth and neighborhood changemakers. Originally distributed in print throughout the North Side, we’re sharing it here so more neighbors and curious minds can engage with the reporting, photography and reflections inside. Whether you live in the North Side or are just getting to know it, we hope this collection deepens your understanding of its 18 neighborhoods and the people shaping it.
