(Photo by Stephanie Strasburg/PublicSource) As recently as July, the Allegheny County Jail was dosing people in its custody with addiction medication in the middle of the night. Advocates called the regimen “triggering” and “detrimental,” and doctors in the community said they wouldn’t treat patients that way.
