(Photo by Stephanie Strasburg/Pittsburgh's Public Source) The Allegheny County Jail’s population has risen since 2022, defying a decade-long trend and the goals of county leaders. It’s been three years since Allegheny County agreed to pay a design firm $700,000 to create plans for a major overhaul — and reduction in size — of its jail.
