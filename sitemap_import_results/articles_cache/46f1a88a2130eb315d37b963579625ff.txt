FROM THE SOURCE PODCAST Hosted by Jourdan Hicks Subscribe and listen on: SEASON 4 Season 4 of Public Source’s From the Source podcast will focus on the youth experience and culture in the Pittsburgh area. Young people are too frequently dismissed in the conversations about issues that matter deeply to how they navigate their lives today and in the future. Through this season, they will have the opportunity to share their complex and nuanced perspectives on issues ranging from school safety and climate change to dating and other coming-of-age concerns. Host Jourdan Hicks is back with a new batch of inclusive, intimate and inspiring interviews. Through this podcast, we will amplify youth voices and provide a useful and compelling resource to parents, educators and people who interact with youth in other ways.
